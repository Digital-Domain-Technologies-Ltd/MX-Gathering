### How to Be Recognized

Make any contribution to MX-Gathering:

1. Submit a pull request (documentation, code, templates)
2. Share valuable insights in discussions
3. Organize or facilitate community events
4. Help others in issues or discussions
5. Provide feedback on proposals

All contributions, no matter how small, are valued and recognized.
